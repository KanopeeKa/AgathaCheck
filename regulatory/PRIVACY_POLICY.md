## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

- **Art. 6(1)(a) — Consent:** For optional data such as profile photos, pet photos, bio text, and non-essential cookies/preferences. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 9).
- **Art. 6(1)(b) — Contractual Necessity:** For data required to provide the core service (account management, pet profiles, health tracking, weight tracking, vet contacts, sharing, organisations, notifications).
- **Art. 6(1)(f) — Legitimate Interest:** For security measures, fraud prevention, and service improvement, where our interests do not override your rights.
- **Art. 6(1)(c) — Legal Obligation:** Where we are required to retain data by applicable law.

## 5. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

1. **Provide the Service:** Create and manage your account, pet profiles, health records, weight tracking, vet contacts, organisations, and sharing features.
2. **Generate Reports:** Create PDF pet reports containing your selected data sections.
3. **Send Notifications:** Deliver in-app notifications for health reminders, family events, and sharing activities.
4. **Manage Subscriptions:** Process subscription entitlements via RevenueCat.
5. **Improve the Service:** Analyse usage patterns to improve functionality (only with consent for non-essential analytics).
6. **Ensure Security:** Authenticate users, prevent fraud, and maintain service integrity.

## 9. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15–22)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

### 9.1 Right of Access (Art. 15)
You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Use the "Export My Data" feature in the App or contact us directly.

### 9.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
You may correct inaccurate personal data at any time through the App's profile and pet editing features.

### 9.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten") (Art. 17)
You may request deletion of your personal data. Use the "Delete Account" feature in the App, which cascades deletion to all associated data (pets, health entries, weight entries, notifications, organisation memberships, etc.).

### 9.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18)
You may request restriction of processing in certain circumstances (e.g., while we verify data accuracy).

### 9.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
You may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (JSON). Use the "Export My Data" feature in the App.

### 9.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)
You may object to processing based on legitimate interest. Where we process data for direct marketing (if applicable), you may object at any time.

### 9.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3))
Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time via the consent preferences in the App. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

### 9.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint (Art. 77)
You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (DPA). For a list of EU DPAs, visit: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/about-edpb/members_en

## 12. Data Security (GDPR Art. 32)

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses TLS/HTTPS.
- **Password hashing:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (via dbcrypt) and never stored in plaintext.
- **Authentication:** JWT-based token authentication with expiry and refresh mechanisms.
- **Access control:** Role-based access within organisations; pet sharing with guardian/shared permissions.
- **Database security:** PostgreSQL with parameterised queries to prevent SQL injection.

- Where the breach is likely to result in a high risk, we will notify affected individuals without undue delay (Art. 34).
